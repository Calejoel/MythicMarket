About Mythic Market

Mythic Market is a collection of low‐supply micro meme coin collectibles on Solana — each inspired by mythical gods and launched based on community votes.

We deploy each token with a fixed 5,000 supply, giving them high volatility, long‐term potential, and collectible scarcity.

Launch prices start low to reward early holders; as supply tightens, scarcity grows—fueling price swings and upside.

Tokens pair in SOL/USDC dual pools via Meteora (or SOL‐only, depending on strategy). Liquidity is locked; buybacks and burns increase rarity.

Collect the gods. Trade the legends. Hold the volatility.
