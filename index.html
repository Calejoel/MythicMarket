About Mythic Market

Mythic Market is a Solana-based launch platform focused on releasing low-supply, themed micro meme coins inspired by gods and legendary figures. Each token has a fixed supply of 5,000, designed to create scarcity and drive price discovery through community participation and organic demand. Liquidity is primarily provided in SOL-only pools, using Meteora, with occasional SOL/USDC or alternative configurations depending on the thematic design of each release. Contract addresses are shared simultaneously across Offical Website, Telegram, Discord, and Twitter to ensure equal access at launch. You will vote on which Gods or legendary figures are released next, contributing to the direction of the platform. Following the initial phase, Mythic Market aims to deliver 1â€“2 launches per week, with NO DELAYS, even as launch formats or mechanics evolve. You are encouraged to conduct their own research (DYOR) before each drop, as changes may occur from coin to coin. While fast paced trading is expected, it is important to note that the fixed 5,000 token supply per coin introduces significant volatility, particularly as supply becomes low. We may also buy back and burn remaining coins at announced periods to keep scarcity. Holding a portion of your portfolio long-term is advised as the available supply diminishes over time and you don't get left to FOMO once it rises exponentially.
