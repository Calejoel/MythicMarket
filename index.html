About Mythic Market

Mythic Market is a collection of low-supply micro meme coin collectibles on Solana — each inspired by mythical gods and launched based on community votes.

We create and deploy each token with a fixed 5,000 supply, giving them high volatility, long-term potential, and collectible scarcity.

Launch prices start low to reward early holders, and as the limited supply tightens, scarcity increases — fueling price swings and upside.

Tokens are paired in SOL/USDC dual pools via Meteora, or SOL-only, depending on the strategy.
All liquidity is locked, and we will announce buybacks and burns to increase rarity and reinforce value.

New coins drop frequently (1–2 a week), and once we're fully rolling, no delays and high community engagement will be rewarded.
There's no presale ever, no insider allocations — just fair, transparent meme coin launches powered by mythology and memes.

Collect the gods. Trade the legends. Hold the volatility.
